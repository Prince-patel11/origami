Origami

My Favorite Hobby Is Origami. Origami is the Japanese art of transforming a flat sheet of paper into a three-dimensional sculpture through folding, without cutting or gluing. While paper was invented in China, the art of folding it developed in Japan, with "ori" meaning "fold" and "kami" meaning "paper".
